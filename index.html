Grandama's secret apple pie

This Apple pie recipe has being in my family for generations and never fails to delight.

ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 table spoon of ground cinamon
  • 6 medium sized apples, pilled and sliced

in structions

  1. pre heat hoven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. in a bowl mix the sugar and cinamon
  3. toss the apples in the sugar-cinamon
